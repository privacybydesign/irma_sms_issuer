Add phone number

On this page you can add a (European) mobile telephone number to your Yivi app.

Do you want to add multiple mobile phone numbers? Then follow these steps for each phone number you want to add.

Check your phone number

Please check the phone number again for confirmation. Choose 'Back' to correct your phone number.

Add phone number

You will receive a text message from Yivi.

Please go through the following steps:
  1. Open the text message sent by Yivi.
  2. Choose the link inside the text message.
  3. You will be redirected back to your Yivi app.
